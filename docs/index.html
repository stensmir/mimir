Features

Built for focus.

No cloud. No subscriptions. No distractions.

Your voice stays yours.

Mimir runs offline, always. No cloud, no analytics, no tracking — nothing leaves your machine. The perfect companion for AI tools like Claude Code, ChatGPT, and beyond. Just speak to create.

Choose your engine.

Two world-class speech recognition engines at your fingertips. NVIDIA Parakeet — state-of-the-art accuracy with automatic punctuation, supporting 25 languages. Whisper by OpenAI — the proven open-source standard. Both run locally, both are free. Pick the one you like best.

Transcribe anything.

Meetings, interviews, voice memos — drop any audio or video file and get accurate text in seconds. WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG — all supported natively. Stop wasting hours on manual transcription. Extract the key insights, analyze, and move on.

Integrate with your AI tools.

Mimir provides a built-in MCP server — connect it to Claude Desktop, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible AI agent. Transcribe audio right from your AI conversations. Let your LLM manage voice-to-text on demand. One config, zero friction.

Parakeet ASR engine

Powered by NVIDIA's Parakeet — state-of-the-art speech recognition running locally on your hardware.

Global hotkey

macOS native. Press your hotkey in any app, speak, and text appears right where your cursor is.

Smart dictation

Auto-punctuation and intelligent formatting. Speak naturally — Mimir handles the rest.

Under the hood

Built for speed. Designed for privacy.

Mimir is written in Rust — fast, lightweight, and resource-efficient. NVIDIA's Parakeet ASR model runs locally via ONNX Runtime, delivering near-instant transcription with automatic punctuation.

Configure a global hotkey or set up a wake word — just say "Start recording" and dictate hands-free, no keyboard needed. Optimized for Apple Silicon, with Windows and Linux on the roadmap.
